Baltimore Sculpture Studio

We’ve created a space where we can sculpt in clay from live models in Baltimore’s Station North art district. We are a community of community-oriented, dedicated sculptors with a wonderful, light-filled studio with heating and air conditioning. We are looking for sculptors to join us as yearly, half-yearly and quarter-yearly members.

We have sculpture sessions with live models Sunday from 1-4pm. Full-time members would have 24–7 access to our air-conditioned studio.

Our vision is to branch out to:

  • Workshops with local artists
  • Workshops with visiting artists
  • Artist talks

There is also a gallery in the building so there may be options to participate in gallery shows
